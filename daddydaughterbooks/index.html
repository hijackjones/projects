A note from the reviewers...

We're not authors or literature professors. We're a dad and his daughter. In an indomitable effort to maintain a close bond, I read to my daughter at night before she goes to bed. I've read to her ever since she was a tiny baby. Someone once told us to read to your children for as long as they'll let you. It'll leave a lasting impression, not only of literature and the value of reading, but also in the quality time spent with each other.
